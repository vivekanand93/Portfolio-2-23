About Text Utils

This website is created using Bootstrap and react it is simple tool for text manipulation like word & character counter uppercase to lowercase & vice versa , remove extra spaces , copy text , theme change.

  1. The First slide shows the homepage where you can enter the text and this site will help you with texts utilities as word and character counter , uppercase to lowercase ,lowercase to uppercase , text manipulation
